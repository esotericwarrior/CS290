Hello! My name is Tristan. I love video games, movies, and education. I'm currently enrolled in Oregon State University's online post-baccalaureate computer science program, while working full-time as an English Teacher in Gunsan, South Korea of the Jeollabuk-do province.

Teaching has long been a passion of mine and although I've been both blessed and humbled to have had the incredible opportunity to teach here in Korea for five years, I have decided that it is time for me to begin pursuing another passion of mine: computer science.

I was accepted into OSU's post-baccalaureate program last fall and have been excitedly working through since. I am incredibly excited about the opportunity to learn about computers and how to continue implementing the latest technologies for the betterment of society.

Though I've only been enrolled for a few quarters now, I feel like I've already learned so much, yet I know I have only scratched the surface. Each day, I find myself looking more into the future, eager to continue honing my skills to someday join the industry and make my own unique contributions.

If you are interested in discussing anything with me, please head over to the contact section and message me anytime! I look forward to hearing from you! Take care!
